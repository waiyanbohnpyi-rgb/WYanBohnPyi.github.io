Triceratops

Triceratops, meaning "three-horned face," was a massive herbivorous dinosaur that roamed North America roughly 68 to 66 million years ago. Weighing up to 12 tons—comparable to two African elephants—it featured a distinctive bony frill and three facial horns used for defense or social display.
